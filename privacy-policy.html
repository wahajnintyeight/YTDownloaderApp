Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how ConvertYT Go ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using ConvertYT Go, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Search queries and video URLs you enter in the App
  • Download preferences and settings
  • App usage preferences and configurations

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features used, screens viewed, time spent in the App
  • Analytics Data: App opens, session duration, crash reports, performance metrics
  • Network Information: IP address, network type (Wi-Fi, cellular), connection status

1.3 Downloaded Content

Videos and audio files you download through the App are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to these files, and they are not transmitted to our servers or third-party services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide, maintain, and improve the App's features and services
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the App and improve user experience
  • Error Tracking: To identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements and support the free version of the App
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Google Firebase Analytics

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns and improve our services. Firebase Analytics collects:

  • App usage statistics (screen views, feature usage)
  • Device information and identifiers
  • User interactions and events

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers and advertising IDs
  • Location data (if permitted)
  • App usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. For more information, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.3 Sentry

We use Sentry for error tracking and performance monitoring. Sentry collects:

  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics
  • Device and app information related to errors

For more information, please review Sentry's Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

3.4 YouTube

Our App allows you to search and download content from YouTube. We do not collect or store your YouTube account information. Your use of YouTube content is subject to YouTube's Terms of Service: https://www.youtube.com/static?template=terms

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Downloaded videos and audio files are stored locally on your device. App preferences and settings are also stored locally. We do not have access to this locally stored data.

4.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4.3 Data Retention

We retain analytics and usage data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can delete downloaded content and app data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing app data through your device settings.

5. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required to search for videos and download content
  • Storage/Media Access: Required to save downloaded videos and audio files to your device
  • Network State: To check internet connectivity and optimize downloads
  • Wake Lock: To continue downloads when the device screen is off
  • Foreground Service: To maintain download progress in the background

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we collect
  • Deletion: You can delete app data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • Analytics: You can disable analytics collection in the App settings (if available)
Note: To opt out of personalized ads on Android, go to Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization. On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking.
